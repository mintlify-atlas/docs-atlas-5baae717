## What is FluidCalendar?

FluidCalendar combines smart automatic task scheduling with a full-featured calendar. It finds optimal time slots for your tasks based on your work hours, energy levels, and existing events — so you spend less time planning and more time executing.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy with Docker">
    Run `docker compose up -d` to start FluidCalendar and PostgreSQL. See the [quick start guide](/quickstart).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your calendar">
    Link your Google Calendar or Outlook account so FluidCalendar can see your existing events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure work hours">
    Set your working days, hours, and energy levels in **Settings → Auto-Schedule**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tasks and schedule">
    Create tasks with estimated durations, then hit **Auto-schedule** to fill your calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>
